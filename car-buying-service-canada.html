What Is a Car Buying Service?

A car buying service takes the entire vehicle purchasing process off your plate. Instead of spending weekends at dealerships, haggling over prices, and wondering if you're getting a fair deal — a professional handles it all for you.

CarBrokerCanada is a dedicated car buying service for Canadians. We work exclusively on your behalf to find, negotiate, and coordinate the purchase of your next vehicle. We're not a dealership. We don't sell cars. We help you buy them — smarter.

What's Included in Our Service

Whether you choose Option A or Option B, here's what you get:

  • Dealer outreach across Canada — We contact multiple dealerships to find your vehicle at the best price
  • Professional price negotiation — Industry experience working deals in your favour
  • Complete Deal Summary — A transparent breakdown of price, fees, and terms before you commit
  • Paperwork coordination — We handle all the back-and-forth with the dealer
  • DocuSign from home — Sign everything remotely, no dealership visit required until pickup
  • Financing referrals — Connections to trusted lending partners if needed

Two Service Options

Option A — "I Know What I Want" — $500 Flat Fee

You already know the make, model, trim, and color you want. We take it from there — contacting dealers, negotiating the price, and coordinating the purchase. Simple and straightforward.

Option B — "Help Me Find the Right Car" — $500 + $500

Not sure what vehicle is right for you? We provide personalized recommendations based on your needs, budget, and lifestyle. Once you've chosen, we handle everything just like Option A. The second $500 is charged only when your vehicle is ready for pickup.

Risk-Free Guarantee

If CarBrokerCanada is unable to source or secure your requested vehicle, your full payment is refunded. You have nothing to lose.

How It Works — Step by Step

  1. Submit your vehicle request and pay the $500 flat fee online
  2. Your dedicated broker contacts you within 24 hours to confirm every detail
  3. We reach out to dealerships across Canada to find your vehicle
  4. We negotiate the best price and terms on your behalf
  5. You receive a Deal Summary with a full price breakdown for your approval
  6. Once approved, we coordinate all paperwork with the dealer
  7. You sign everything via DocuSign from the comfort of your home
  8. You visit the dealership to pick up your new vehicle
