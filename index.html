Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

General Policy

The $500 service fee paid to CarBrokerCanada is non-refundable. This fee compensates your dedicated broker for the time and effort invested in dealer outreach, price research, negotiation, and coordination on your behalf.

Exception — Vehicle Not Sourced

There is one exception to this policy: if CarBrokerCanada is unable to source or secure the requested vehicle, the full amount paid will be refunded.

This applies in the following situations:

  • The specific vehicle requested (make, model, trim, color) is not available from any dealership in our network
  • A deal cannot be reached with any dealership on terms that meet the client's approved criteria
  • CarBrokerCanada determines that the vehicle cannot be secured within a reasonable timeframe

Refund Process

If a refund is warranted, CarBrokerCanada will notify the client and process the refund to the original payment method within 10 business days.

Non-Refundable Situations

The fee is not refundable if:

  • The client changes their mind after the service has begun
  • The client rejects a deal that meets their stated criteria
  • The client becomes unresponsive during the process
  • The client provides inaccurate information that affects the search

Option B — Second Payment

For Option B clients, the second $500 fee is charged only when the vehicle is confirmed and ready for pickup. If the vehicle cannot be sourced, neither the first nor second payment will be retained.
